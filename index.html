About Me

Hi! My name is Robert Boyd, but you can call me Bobby. I am an aspiring full stack web developer. I have a degree in Mass Communications from California State University, Sacramento. However, I have always been fascinated by web development ever since my highschool web class. Because of this fascination, I decided to embark on my web development journey.

My web development journey started when I got a job in IT. At my new IT job, well, I wasn't actually doing much work. Business was slow, and I was bored. So, I decided to remake their website, and from then on I knew that's what I wanted to do.
I remade their website first using Wordpress, however when that was done I decided that I wanted more.

After I finished the new Wordpress website, I decided that I wanted to learn React. Not knowing how big of a step that was going to be, I watched Youtube video after Youtube video until I remade their website again. My new found fascination for React led me to enroll in a coding bootcamp. At the bootcamp I am ready to hone my coding skills and prepare myself for a web development career.
